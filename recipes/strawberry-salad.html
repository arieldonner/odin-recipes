Strawberry Salad

Description

A refreshing salad that is the perfect appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romain lettuce, cut or torn into bite sized pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) package shredded cabbage
  • 1 1/2 cups golden raisins
  • 1 cup red grapes, halved
  • 1 pint strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle creamy poppy seed salad dressing
  • 2 (2.25 ounce) packages pine nuts
  • 2 (2.45 ounce packages slice almonds

Steps

  1. Mix the lettuce, cabbage, raisins, grapes, strawberries, and onion together in a large bowl.
  2. Add the dressing and toss to coat.
  3. Top with the pine nuts and almonds to serve.
